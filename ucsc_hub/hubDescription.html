Human rRNA Modifications

RNA modification tracks for human rRNA sequences (18S, 28S, 5.8S, 5S).

Track colors encode both modification type (hue) and stoichiometry (saturation). Vivid colors indicate highly modified sites; pale colors indicate partially modified sites.

Score

The score (shading intensity) reflects detection confidence, rescaled to 0-1000 per file. Darker = higher confidence. Note: scores are rescaled independently per file and are not directly comparable across tracks.

Consensus track

The Consensus track merges the sample and filtered tracks. Colors use a sequential red scale based on how often the modification is observed:

ObservationColor
1 / 2 samplesLight red
2 / 2 samplesDark red

Extra fields (visible on click)

Clicking any site in the browser shows coverage (raw read depth) and frequency (% of reads showing the modification).
